Pricing Philosophy
Annual commitment. Meaningful change.

Program fees are annual and cover onboarding, core lab panels, clinician time, program management, and ongoing monitoring. Medication costs — particularly GLP-1 therapies — are typically a separate line item passed through at cost, so the program fee reflects the care relationship, not the drug.

Transparent pricing: All fees shown are in CAD and reflect the program membership only. Labs and medications may vary by case and are disclosed upfront at intake. Think of these as "starting at" prices — your physician will confirm total costs at your initial visit.

A note on medication costs
Program fees are structured to cover the care relationship — physician time, lab panels, monitoring, and portal access. Medication costs are always separated and disclosed upfront. For GLP-1 programs especially, drug costs (often $2,400–$5,000+ per year out-of-pocket) can exceed the program fee itself. We believe in transparent pricing: you'll always know what the program costs and what the medication costs before you commit.
